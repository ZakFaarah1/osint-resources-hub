# Eliot Higgins

## Who They Are
Eliot Higgins is the founder of Bellingcat and a leading figure in open-source intelligence. He is known for using publicly available information to investigate global conflicts, war crimes, and disinformation campaigns.

## Why They Matter
Eliot Higgins helped pioneer modern OSINT by demonstrating that independent researchers can conduct investigations traditionally performed by intelligence agencies. His work has influenced journalism, cybersecurity, and threat intelligence.

---

## Notable Work
- MH17 investigation  
- Syrian chemical weapons analysis  
- Russian military and disinformation investigations  

---

## Techniques and Approach
- Social media analysis  
- Geolocation of images and videos  
- Cross-referencing multiple sources  
- Timeline reconstruction  

---

## Impact
- Helped bring OSINT into mainstream journalism  
- Demonstrated the value of public data in investigations  
- Contributed to exposing disinformation campaigns

## Why I Follow Them
Eliot Higgins shows that impactful investigations do not require classified data, but rather the ability to analyze and verify open information. His work aligns with my interests in cybersecurity and intelligence.
