# OSINT and Cybersecurity Rabbit Holes

## AI-Generated Phishing

- AI can generate highly realistic phishing emails  
- Personalization at scale makes detection more difficult  
- Directly related to modern phishing and automation trends  

---

## Deepfakes and Disinformation

- Synthetic media can be used to manipulate public perception  
- Increasingly used in political and geopolitical contexts  
- Creates challenges for verification and trust  

---

## Geolocation Techniques

- Identifying locations using:
  - Shadows  
  - Terrain  
  - Buildings  
- Commonly used in OSINT investigations and challenges

## Data Exhaust

- Every digital action leaves a trace  
- Social media, metadata, and logs can be used for investigations  
- Valuable for building intelligence from public data
