# Melissa (Melissa Data)

## Link
https://www.melissa.com

---

## What It Does
Melissa is a data intelligence and enrichment platform that allows you to perform lookups on emails, phone numbers, names, and business data to validate and expand information.

---

## Why It Matters
Melissa is especially useful in OSINT for initial probing because it helps turn limited or partial information into structured, validated data that can be used for deeper investigation.

---

## Key Lookup Tools

- Email Lookup  
- Phone Lookup  
- Name / Identity Lookup  
- Business Lookup  
- IP and domain-related enrichment (limited compared to other tools)

---

## Step-by-Step Example 1: Email Lookup

### Goal
Validate an email and extract useful metadata.

### Steps
1. Go to Melissa
2. Select Email Check or Email Lookup
3. Enter the email address
4. Review results for:
   - domain validity
   - mailbox confidence
   - email type (business, personal, disposable)
   - potential risk indicators

### Why This Helps
- Confirms if an email is likely real
- Helps determine whether it belongs to a legitimate organization
- Can guide further searches on domain or username

---

## Step-by-Step Example 2: Phone Lookup

### Goal
Gather information tied to a phone number.

### Steps
1. Open the phone lookup tool
2. Enter the phone number
3. Review results for:
   - geographic location
   - carrier information
   - line type (mobile, VoIP, landline)

### Why This Helps
- Identifies whether a number is real or VoIP
- Helps determine location and context
- Useful in identifying potential burner numbers

---

## Step-by-Step Example 3: Name or Identity Lookup

### Goal
Expand limited personal information into usable intelligence.

### Steps
1. Enter a name or partial identity into a lookup tool
2. Review results for:
   - possible address associations
   - related records
   - data consistency
3. Cross-reference results with search engines or social media

### Why This Helps
- Builds a starting profile from minimal data
- Helps confirm whether a person or identity is real
- Supports correlation across multiple data points

---

## Step-by-Step Example 4: Initial Probing Workflow

### Scenario
You only have:
- an email  
- a phone number  
- or a name  

### Steps
1. Run the data through Melissa lookup tools
2. Extract validated and structured information
3. Use results to pivot into:
   - search engines  
   - social media tools  
   - domain reconnaissance  
4. Build connections between identifiers

### Why This Helps
Melissa acts as a **data enrichment layer** that improves the quality of your starting point before deeper OSINT analysis.

---

## Use Cases

- Validating emails and phone numbers  
- Enriching partial identity data  
- Identifying whether data is legitimate or suspicious  
- Supporting early-stage reconnaissance  

---

## Notes

- Some features require an account or API access  
- Results depend on input quality  
- Best used alongside other OSINT tools for correlation  
- Not a replacement for deeper investigation tools  

---

## Key Insight

Melissa is most effective at the beginning of an investigation, where it helps transform raw or uncertain data into something reliable and actionable.
