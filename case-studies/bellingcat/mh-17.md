# MH17 Investigation (Bellingcat)

## Source
https://www.bellingcat.com/news/europe/2020/03/09/the-impossible-conspiracy-fuelling-russias-mh17-defence/

---

## Overview
The MH17 investigation is one of the most well-known examples of open-source intelligence being used to support a major international investigation. Malaysia Airlines Flight MH17 was shot down over eastern Ukraine in 2014.

---

## Techniques Used

- Social Media Analysis  
  Collected images and videos posted by civilians and identified key details such as vehicles and locations  

- Geolocation  
  Matched landmarks in photos and videos to real-world locations and verified movement routes  

- Image Verification  
  Compared multiple sources to confirm authenticity and identified unique markings  

- Timeline Reconstruction  
  Ordered events using timestamps and visual evidence to track movement before and after the attack  

---

## Key Findings

- The missile system was a Buk launcher  
- It originated from Russian military units  
- It was transported into Ukraine and later returned to Russia  

---

## Tools and Methods Involved

- Satellite imagery (Google Earth)  
- Reverse image searching  
- Open-source video analysis  
- Metadata and timestamp verification  

---

## Why It Matters

- Demonstrates that non-government investigators can conduct high-level intelligence analysis  
- Shows how OSINT can support legal and international accountability  
- Highlights the value of publicly available data  

---

## Key Lessons

- OSINT can be as powerful as classified intelligence when used correctly  
- Verification across multiple sources is critical  
- Small details can lead to major breakthroughs  

---

## Relevance to Cybersecurity and AI

- Large datasets such as images, videos, and social media can be analyzed for intelligence  
- Similar techniques apply to threat intelligence and misinformation analysis  

---

## My Insight

This case study shows that the real power of OSINT is not just access to data, but the ability to organize, verify, and analyze it effectively. It also highlights how modern events leave digital traces that can be reconstructed by skilled analysts.
