# Discord Communities (OSINT & Cybersecurity)

Discord is one of the most active platforms for real-time collaboration in OSINT and cybersecurity. Many communities host live discussions, challenges, and investigations.

---

## OSINT-Focused Communities

### Project Owl
- Link: https://discord.com/invite/projectowl
- Focus: Real-time global event monitoring, crisis response, OSINT collaboration
- Why join:
  - Large and active community
  - Real-world OSINT applications
  - Good networking opportunities

---

### Bellingcat Community
- Link: https://discord.gg/pCB9Xn8fK4
- Focus: Investigative journalism, geolocation, war/conflict analysis
- Why join:
  - High-quality OSINT techniques
  - Real investigation case discussions
  - Strong analytical approach

---

## Hybrid (OSINT + Cybersecurity)

### NahamSec
- Link: https://discord.gg/zFPqmCQJgy
- Focus: Bug bounty, reconnaissance, hacking techniques
- Why join:
  - Strong recon skills development
  - Active community with practical knowledge

---

### Threat Hunter Community
- Link: https://discord.gg/threathunter
- Focus: Threat intelligence, SOC workflows, detection
- Why join:
  - Aligns with blue team and SOC roles
  - Useful for learning detection techniques

---

### DEF CON Community
- Link: https://discord.gg/defcon
- Focus: General hacking, research, conference discussions
- Why join:
  - One of the largest hacker communities
  - Exposure to multiple domains (OSINT, red team, hardware)

---

## Notes

- Discord communities are highly dynamic and links may expire
- Best approach:
  1. Join 1–2 major servers (Project Owl, NahamSec)
  2. Network and discover smaller or private communities
  3. Participate in challenges to build credibility
